Privacy Policy for TearReader

Last Updated: 1/7/2026

TearReader ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Local File Processing

TearReader is designed with privacy as a priority. When you open files (PDF, EPUB, TXT, or MD) within the app, all processing occurs locally on your device. We do not upload your documents, books, or reading materials to any external server. Your personal library remains private and stays on your phone.

Web Article Extraction

If you use the "Web" feature to read articles from a URL, the URL you provide is sent to a third-party CORS proxy service (AllOrigins) to fetch the HTML content. This is necessary to bypass browser security restrictions (CORS) and extract the text for speed reading. The text content returned is processed locally on your device. We do not store a history of the websites you visit.
